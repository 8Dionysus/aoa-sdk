# Agon Recurrence Adapter

The Agon Recurrence Adapter teaches the recurrence control plane to observe Agon surfaces from Waves 0–VI.

It is an adapter, not a scheduler.

It reads owner-owned recurrence component manifests and hook binding sets. It can support review queues, candidate dossiers, and owner summaries. It cannot decide Agon outcomes.

## Inputs

- Center Agon law surfaces from `Agents-of-Abyss`.
- Actor and assistant formation surfaces from `aoa-agents`.
- Gate routing surfaces from `aoa-routing`.
- Trial playbook surfaces from `aoa-playbooks`.
- Technique binding candidates from `aoa-techniques`.
- Skill binding candidates from `aoa-skills`.

## Outputs

- Adapter registry.
- Review queue examples.
- Candidate dossier examples.
- Owner summary examples.

## Stop-line

Recurrence observes recurrence. It does not become the arena.
