# Swarm Ledger Closeout Helper Contracts Contract

## Contract

The part validates SDK-visible Titan swarm and closeout helper surfaces without
turning reports, grades, or memory candidates into stronger truth.

## SDK-Owned Active Names

- part route: `titan/swarm-ledger-closeout-helper-contracts`
- docs: `docs/swarm-ledger.md`, `docs/closeout-audit.md`
- script: `scripts/titan_swarm.py`
- schemas: task, report, finding, grade, timeout, ledger, and closeout schemas
- examples: task and report examples
- tests: `tests/test_titan_swarm_ledger.py`, `tests/test_titan_closeout_audit.py`

## Stop-Lines

- Do not treat a Titan report as memory or proof.
- Do not treat closeout audit grades as owner verdicts.
- Do not keep swarm/closeout helper artifacts in root technical districts.
