# Experience Roadmap

Experience is the SDK helper-contract route for experience-shaped workflows.
It supports capture, adoption, deployment, governance, and office release-train
surfaces without claiming owner execution, consent, or release acceptance.

## Current Contour

- Keep capture pipeline, adoption federation, deployment watchtower, governance
  runtime, and office release-train helper contracts routed through active
  `parts/`.
- Keep helper contracts explicit about input, output, owner, next route, and
  what the SDK may safely inspect.
- Keep deployment and governance helpers below owner consent and runtime truth.
- Keep office release-train helpers as support contracts, not release
  authority.

## Next Work

- Tighten helper contracts where repeated workflow pressure has stable fields
  and a clear owner route.
- Keep generated companions out until a helper source contract is stable.
- Route owner-local adoption, office, deployment, and governance acceptance to
  the repositories that can accept them.

## When Time Comes

- Add a new Experience part when repeated workflow pressure needs its own helper
  contract and validation route.
- Add generated companions only after source contracts and focused tests stay
  stable.
- Widen helper behavior only after consent, rollback, and owner acceptance
  posture are explicit.

## Out Of Scope

- Experience governance authority.
- Deployment truth.
- Office execution truth.
- Owner consent or release acceptance.
- Live workspace runtime activation.
