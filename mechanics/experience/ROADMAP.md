# Experience Roadmap

This roadmap owns future pressure for the SDK Experience mechanic.

## Current Contour

Experience owns helper contracts for capture pipeline, adoption federation,
deployment watchtower, governance runtime, and office release-train routes.
These helpers describe bounded SDK contracts around Experience surfaces without
claiming owner execution.

## Next Work

- Keep helper contracts explicit about input, output, owner, and next route.
- Keep deployment and governance helpers below owner consent and runtime truth.
- Keep office release-train helpers as support contracts, not as release
  authority.

## When Time Comes

- Add a new Experience part only when repeated workflow pressure needs its own
  helper contract and validation route.
- Add generated companions only after the helper source contract is stable.

## Out Of Scope

- Experience governance authority;
- deployment truth;
- office execution truth;
- owner consent or release acceptance.

## Update Trigger

Update this roadmap when Experience helper contract scope, owner boundary, or
future workflow support conditions change.
