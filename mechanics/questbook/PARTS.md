# Questbook Parts

## Candidate Parts

| Part | Role |
| --- | --- |
| `quest-source-store` | root `quests/` source record placement and lane/state law |
| `public-obligation-index` | root `QUESTBOOK.md` human open-obligation index posture |
| `lifecycle-dispatch-posture` | lifecycle vocabulary and future generated-reader posture |

## Part Boundary

Questbook parts own route law and validation posture. They do not own the
helper payloads referenced by quest records; those stay in their active
mechanic parts.
