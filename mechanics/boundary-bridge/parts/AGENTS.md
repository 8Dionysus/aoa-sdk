## Boundary Bridge Parts Route

Use these parts when the SDK exposes a typed bridge over source-owned sibling
surfaces without taking their meaning.

Active part homes:

- `consumed-surface-posture-gate/` checks whether sibling-owned surfaces are
  safe to read before SDK facades trust them.
- `skill-runtime-bridge/` keeps skill detection, dispatch, host availability,
  and runtime session reporting below `aoa-skills` meaning.
- `technique-promotion-readiness-reader/` keeps promotion-readiness reads below
  `aoa-techniques` meaning and review authority.
- `owner-layer-signal-handoff/` keeps advisory surface detection and reviewed
  owner-layer handoff material below activation or dispatch authority.

Keep source-owned truth in sibling repositories. SDK parts may expose handles,
labels, and reviewed handoff packets only.
