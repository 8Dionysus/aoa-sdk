# Surface Path Transport Contract

## Allowed Outputs

- Expected owner surface IDs.
- Expected generated relative paths.
- Expected collection wrapper shapes.
- Fragment-ref posture for item lookup.

## Stop-Lines

- Do not rewrite owner refs silently.
- Do not make staging wrappers owner doctrine.
- Do not claim runtime or frontend delivery authority.

## Owner Split

The SDK records consumer path expectations. `Agents-of-Abyss`, `abyss-stack`,
and other owner repositories keep source, generated, runtime, and presentation
truth.
