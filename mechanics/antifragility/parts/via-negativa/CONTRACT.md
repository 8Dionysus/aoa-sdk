# Via Negativa Contract

## Allowed Outputs

- Keep route.
- Merge route.
- Move route.
- Suppression or quarantine note.
- Deprecation or removal candidate.
- Provenance-preserving owner handoff.

## Stop-Lines

- Do not delete source evidence that must be retained.
- Do not approve owner-local deletion from the SDK.
- Do not replace proof review with cleanup wording.

## Owner Split

The SDK asks pruning questions for SDK helper surfaces. Owner repositories
decide actual removal, replacement, proof, and local policy.
