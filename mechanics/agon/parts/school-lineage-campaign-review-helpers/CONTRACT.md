# Agon School Lineage Campaign Review Helpers Contract

## Function

Build and validate SLC SDK helper candidates.

## May

- load the part-local SLC seed;
- generate a deterministic compact registry;
- validate schema shape, stop-lines, candidate-only status, and non-authority
  posture;
- export reviewable candidate objects.

## Must Not

- activate campaigns;
- canonize schools or lineages;
- issue verdicts, scars, rank, retention, KAG promotion, or ToS promotion;
- schedule hidden actions;
- treat old root paths as active routes.

Executable proof lives in [VALIDATION](VALIDATION.md).
