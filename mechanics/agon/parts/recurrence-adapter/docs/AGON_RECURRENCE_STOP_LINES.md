# Agon Recurrence Stop-Lines

The adapter is review-plane wiring only.

It may prepare:

```text
observations
beacons
review queues
candidate dossiers
owner summaries
wiring plans
```

It may not prepare:

```text
arena sessions
sealed commits
verdicts
scar writes
retention schedules
rank mutations
Tree-of-Sophia promotions
hidden scheduler actions
automatic owner rewrites
```
