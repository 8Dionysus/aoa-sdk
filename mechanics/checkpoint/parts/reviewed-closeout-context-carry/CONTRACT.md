# Reviewed Closeout Context Carry Contract

## Allowed Outputs

- Checkpoint lineage hints.
- Reviewed candidate lineage maps.
- Reviewed owner followthrough maps.
- Component drift hints and refresh followthrough decisions.
- Reviewed continuity windows.
- Reviewed next-kernel followthrough decisions.

## Stop-Lines

- Do not mint `candidate_ref`, `seed_ref`, `object_ref`, continuity truth, or
  owner acceptance from the SDK.
- Do not treat component refresh hints as completed owner refresh receipts.
- Do not execute the recommended next kernel skill from the carry packet.
- Do not let memo, stats, playbook, proof, or runtime projections become
  source truth.

## Owner Split

The SDK keeps carry explicit and schema-checked. Stronger owner repositories
decide landing, repair, refresh, memory, proof, progression, continuity, and
execution.
