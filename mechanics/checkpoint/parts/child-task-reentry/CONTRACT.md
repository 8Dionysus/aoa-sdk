# Child-Task Reentry Contract

- The SDK owns only its bounded child-task transport schemas. Capability
  meaning and workflow execution remain with their owners.
- Codex-local targets may read `aoa-agents` projection manifests but do not own
  role projection defaults.
- Return and checkpoint evidence packets stay mechanical until reviewed owner
  routes accept them.
- Runtime return evidence receipts are candidates for owner review, not live
  automation or proof of capability execution.
- Request and result payloads use exact `capability_refs`; they do not carry
  SDK-selected skill names.
- The dry-run fixture must keep `dry_run=true` and `live_automation=false`.
- Result packets and reviewed handoffs must keep
  `capability_execution_claimed=false`; no legacy runtime-wave alias is kept.
