# Child-Task Reentry Contract

- The SDK consumes `aoa-summon` schemas; it does not redefine summon law.
- Codex-local targets may read `aoa-agents` projection manifests but do not own
  role projection defaults.
- Return and checkpoint bridge packets stay mechanical until reviewed owner
  routes accept them.
- Runtime return closeout receipts are candidates for owner publication, not
  live automation.
- The dry-run fixture must keep `dry_run=true` and `live_automation=false`.
